Byomkesh Bakshi

About the character

Byomkesh Bakshi is a fictional detective character created by Sharadindu Bandyopadhyay. He is known for his keen intellect and sharp observational skills, solving complex mysteries in Calcutta during the 1930s and 1940s.

Author

Byomkesh Bakshi was brought to life by Sharadindu Bandyopadhyay, a renowned Bengali writer. The character first appeared in the short story "Satyanweshi" published in 1932.
